- **14+/20** = Good (70%+) - Minimum competitive
- **15+/20** = Very Good (75%+) - Strong candidate
- **16+/20** = Excellent (80%+) - Top candidate

### Application Strategy
1. **Apply to 3-5 universities** (mix of reach and safe options)
