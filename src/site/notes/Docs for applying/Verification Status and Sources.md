## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Disclaimers

### Information Accuracy
- All information was verified as of November 2025
- **University requirements can change** - always verify current requirements on official websites before applying
- Some universities update requirements annually
- Deadlines and fees are subject to change

### Language Requirements
- Requirements may vary by specific program
- Some universities accept multiple language certificates
- Always check the official program page for your specific program

### GPA Requirements
- **Only Politecnico di Milano** officially states minimum GPA (15.5/20 for Iran)
- All other universities evaluate holistically
- Competitive applicants typically have 14-15/20 (70-75%) in Iranian system

### Tuition Fees
- Public universities: Based on family income (ISEE/ISEEU calculation)
- Fees are approximate and subject to change
- Non-EU students use ISEEU Parificato for income calculation
