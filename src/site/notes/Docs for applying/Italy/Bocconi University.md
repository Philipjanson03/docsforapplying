- **Minimum GPA:** Not officially specified (typically competitive, 14-15/20 in Iranian system)

### Language Requirements:
- **IELTS Academic:** 6.5 minimum overall, with at least 6.0 in each section

- **Duolingo English Test:** 110 minimum (also accepted)

- **Note:** Language certificate must be provided by enrollment deadline (typically June 30)
