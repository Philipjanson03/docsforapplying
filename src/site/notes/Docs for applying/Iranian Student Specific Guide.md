## üèõÔ∏è Italian Embassy Process in Iran

### Italian Embassy in Tehran

**Location:** Tehran (check current address on embassy website)
**Services:** Visa processing, Document legalization, Study abroad support

**Key Contact Points:**
- **Visa Section:** Handles study visa applications
- **Cultural Section:** Information on scholarships, Italian culture
- **Consular Section:** Document legalization

### Pre-Enrollment (Universitaly) Process

**MANDATORY for all Iranian students:**

1. **Register on Universitaly:** https://www.universitaly.it/
2. **Upload documents** (degree, transcripts, etc.)
3. **Pre-enroll** in chosen university/program
4. **Submit to embassy** (either online or in-person)
5. **Deadline:** April 29, 2026 (for Fall 2026 entry)

**This is separate from university application!** You must do BOTH.

**Cost:** Expect ‚Ç¨20-50 per page

### Declaration of Value (Dichiarazione di Valore)

**What it is:** Italian Embassy certification that your Iranian degree is legitimate

**How to get it:**
1. Submit original degree + transcripts to Italian Embassy Tehran
2. Embassy verifies with IAUT
3. Embassy issues Declaration of Value
4. Use this document for university enrollment

**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks (START EARLY!)
**Cost:** Check with embassy
**Note:** Some universities accept "Attestation of Comparability" instead

## üí≥ Financial Considerations

### Banking Challenges

**Due to sanctions, Iranian cards don't work internationally.**

**Solutions:**
1. **Exchange offices in Iran:**
   - Convert Rial to Euro before leaving
   - Carry cash (within legal limits)
   - Safe but inconvenient

2. **Friends/family abroad:**
   - Someone outside Iran can hold account
   - Transfer money through them
   - Common solution for Iranian students

3. **Hawala/Money transfer services:**
   - Traditional money transfer networks
   - Available in Iran and Italy
   - Check legality and rates

4. **Italian bank account:**
   - Open upon arrival in Italy
   - Use for tuition, living expenses
   - Can receive transfers from Iran via intermediary banks

5. **Cryptocurrencies:**
   - Some students use as intermediate
   - Convert to Euro in Italy
   - Understand risks and regulations

### Proving Financial Means for Visa

**Requirement:** Show ‚Ç¨6,000-‚Ç¨8,000 per year

**Options:**
1. **Iranian bank statement:**
   - Convert Rial amount to Euro
   - Get official letter from bank in English
   - Show sufficient funds

2. **Sponsor abroad:**
   - Family member outside Iran with bank account
   - Sponsorship letter
   - Their bank statements

3. **Scholarship letter:**
   - If awarded MAECI or university scholarship
   - Reduces financial proof needed
   - Best option!

## ‚úàÔ∏è Travel & Visa

### Study Visa Application

**Process:**
1. Get admission letter from university
2. Complete Universitaly pre-enrollment
3. Apply for study visa at Italian Embassy Tehran
4. Submit all documents
5. Attend interview (if required)
6. Wait for processing
7. Receive visa

**Processing Time:** 4-8 weeks (can be longer from Iran)
**Apply:** As soon as you have admission (June 2026)
**Deadline:** June 30, 2026

**Required for Visa:**
- Admission letter
- Financial proof (‚Ç¨6,000-8,000)
- Accommodation proof
- Health insurance
- Valid passport
- Passport photos
- Application form
- Visa fee payment

### Travel to Italy

**No Direct Flights:** Iran ‚Üí Italy requires connections

**Common Routes:**
1. **Via Turkey:**
   - Tehran ‚Üí Istanbul ‚Üí Italian city
   - Turkish Airlines, Pegasus
   - Usually cheapest
   - 6-8 hours total

2. **Via UAE:**
   - Tehran ‚Üí Dubai ‚Üí Italian city
   - Emirates, FlyDubai
   - Comfortable, more expensive
   - 8-10 hours total

3. **Via Qatar:**
   - Tehran ‚Üí Doha ‚Üí Italian city
   - Qatar Airways
   - Good service, mid-price
   - 8-10 hours total

**Booking Tips:**
- Book after visa approval (refundable tickets expensive)
- Student discounts available
- September is busy season (book early)
- Check baggage allowance (you'll have lots!)

**Luggage:**
- Maximum 30kg usually (check airline)
- Bring essentials, buy rest in Italy
- Important: medications, documents, clothes
- Can ship some items separately if needed

## üéì Academic Culture Differences

### Iranian vs Italian Universities

**Similarities:**

- Importance of exams
- Respect for professors

**Differences:**

- Participate actively
- Form study groups
- Use office hours
- Start assignments early
- Ask questions
- Don't memorize, understand!
