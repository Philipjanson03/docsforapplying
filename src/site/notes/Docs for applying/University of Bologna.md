## üìã Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements:
- **Bachelor's Degree:** Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Mathematics, Engineering, or related field
- **Minimum GPA:** Not officially specified (typically 70/100 or 14/20 Iranian system recommended)
- **Prerequisites:** Strong background in:
  - Programming (Python, Java, C++)
  - Mathematics (Linear Algebra, Calculus, Statistics)
  - Computer Science fundamentals

### Language Requirements:
- **IELTS Academic:** 5.5-6.5 (range accepted)
- **TOEFL iBT:** 72-94
- **PTE Academic:** 59-75
- **English Level:** B2 CEFR minimum
- **Other:** Cambridge, Duolingo also accepted

**Note:** Bologna accepts wider range of English tests and scores compared to other universities
