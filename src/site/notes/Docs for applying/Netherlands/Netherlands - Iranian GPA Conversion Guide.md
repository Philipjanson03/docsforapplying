## üìã Conversion Methodology

### How Dutch Universities Convert Grades:

1. **Nuffic Standards:** Dutch universities use Nuffic (Dutch organisation for internationalisation) conversion standards
2. **Percentage-Based:** Often convert to percentage first, then to Dutch 1-10 scale
3. **Holistic Evaluation:** Most universities evaluate holistically, not just GPA
4. **Subject-Specific:** Some universities (like TU/e) place heavy weight on specific subjects (Math, CS)

### Iranian to Dutch Conversion Formula:

**Approximate Formula:**
- Iranian 20-point scale ‚Üí Dutch 10-point scale
