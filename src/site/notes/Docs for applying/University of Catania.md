- **Minimum GPA:** Not officially specified (typically 14-15/20 in Iranian system for competitive admission)
- **Prerequisites:** Strong background in CS fundamentals, programming, mathematics

### Language Requirements:

**For English-Taught Programs:**
- **IELTS Academic:** 6.0 minimum (typical requirement for English programs)
- **TOEFL iBT:** 74-80 minimum (typical range)
- **English Level:** B2 CEFR minimum
- **Note:** Specific requirements may vary by program. Consult official program page for exact requirements.

**For Italian-Taught Programs:**
- **Italian Language:** B2 level required
- **CISIA L2 Italian Test:** Minimum score of 40/72
- **Note:** Non-EU students must demonstrate Italian proficiency for Italian-taught programs
